The Beginning

Venkat and Rajesh started FACE on an exceptionally sunny day in the December of 2008.

What started as a mission to upskill the new recruits soon became a revolution. We were joined by the best minds in the country as we set out to make a training culture that had never been seen before.

The Realization

We realized early that the traditional learning and development methods rely on conformity, and would soon be obsolete. We made empathy and learning our battle weapons, and set out to make professional excellence a way of life. With a large millennial workforce, we became the natural go-to people to train the new recruits.

The Present

We are working with some of India’s best businesses. Our training content modules, delivery models and results are exceptional. Our large millennial workforce loves the freedom and learning they experience with us.

We onboard new recruits. We conduct process training for front line executives. We deliver high impact workshops to improve productivity, efficiency and quality of life for people.

We conduct these unique training experiences in more than 1000 cities. With world class trainers and a growing customer base, FACE is the easiest way for businesses to up-skill people they care about.

CULTURE

Our culture is built around the people who work here, and we all work hard to make FACE’s culture exceptional. We obsess over details and value relationships. We party yet consistently exceed expectations. A strong emphasis on learning, hustling, and team work allows us to perform our best every single day, while having fun.

We provide employee perks like flexible work hours, open talking zones, unlimited coffee breaks and training opportunities. We strive to keep our employees happy and healthy by playing games, conducting team outings and celebrating birthdays together.
