Campus to Corporate Program

Graduates enter a company with different expectations. Some want to work hard and earn their place, some want to make an impact, and others want to learn new things every day. They are often unsure of what to expect, and feel lost. Their productivity, efficiency and effectiveness in the Company, for a large part, depends on the induction and orientation they receive in the first few weeks.

The Millennials have shorter attention spans, crave social components, and thrive on instant gratification. Our training modules are tailor made to suit their temperament and have proven to be highly effective, time and again.

During the course of this program, the new recruits adapt to 16 hour days for 6 days a week. We instil good values, sense of belonging, responsibility and discipline in them. They get up at 5 AM and do Yoga. There are team activities, games and writing workshops. They learn corporate etiquette, intra-team communication skills, stress management and team spirit.

They transition from a college attitude to a high intensity corporate environment. We make them understand your company and culture.
