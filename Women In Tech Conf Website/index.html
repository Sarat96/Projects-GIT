About the Summit.

Women In Technology (WIT) summit is being organised first time by School of Information Technology and Engineering (SITE) at VIT Vellore, Tamilnadu, India. Women in Technology summit combines engaging and inspirational speakers with diverse and interactive technology topics.  It will offer a unique opportunity to learn, share and network with Educators, Scientists, Developers, Researchers and Leaders from Industries, Universities and Scientific & Research bodies from all over India

The WIT summit will be providing platform to have candid conversations to discuss common challenges and establish real strategic solutions to grow your career in technology

Join us for a full-day summit with focused topics and panels to get inspired, learn about the latest trends in technology, and network with others during WIT-summit

Who Are We.

VIT.

Vellore Institute Technology popularly known as VIT was established with the aim of providing quality higher education on par with International Standards. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education on a consistent basis. The technological institute is a major, comprehensive and student-centred research institute dedicated to excellence in teaching, research and service.

The green campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere with students from all corners of the globe. Experienced and learned teachers are strongly devoted to nurture the students. The global standards set at VIT in the field of teaching and research spurs us our relentless pursuit of excellence. In fact, it has become a way of life for us

The highly motivated youngsters on the campus are a constant source of pride. Our major strength is the Memoranda of Understanding with various international Universities for global exposure. They provide for an exchange of students and faculty and encourage towards joining research project for mutual benefit of these Universities. VITians who pursue their research in foreign Universities gets high reputation for their innovative work, which brings laurels to India and makes us proud. As a Technical Educational Institution offering state-of-the-art facilities to students from India and abroad, VIT is fast blossoming into an Institution of International repute

SITE.

The School of Information Technology & Engineering (SITE) emphasizes the fields of Information Technology, Software Engineering and Domain Specific Applications so as to facilitate the evolution of skills in students to help them attain a higher degree of knowledge, global competency and excellence, for the betterment of the society.

The Research and instructional programs strive to establish a strong component of practical engagement with private enterprises. This school offers BCA, B.Tech (I.T), MCA, M.Tech (I.T), B.Sc(Multimedia and Animation) and Ph.D., programmes. Major areas of research include Computer Networks, Parallel Architecture, Software Engineering, Data Mining, Cloud Computing, Intelligence Systems and Computer Graphics. It has highly qualified faculty members and distinguished professionals from industry and eminent researchers. The academic programmes of the School have been designed to cater to the ever changing needs and demands of the IT industry.

The focus is on holistic learning and empowering students to make significant contributions at all levels. We have totally 180 faculty in which 88 are female faculties who are highly qualified and received degrees from reputed national and international institutes
