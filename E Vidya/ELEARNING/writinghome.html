Writing Course - 01

Writing is a skill that needs clear and specific expressions. The reason is that, the writer is not in person to explain the doubts of the reader. So, the words chosen and most importantly the order of the words should be in such a way that it does not lead to any confusion in the meaning.

Here are a few sentences that have two or more meanings.

Scenario 1 : The man hit the boy with a book.
This sentence can mean –
1) the man hit the boy using a book.
2) the man hit the boy who was having a book in his hand.

Thus, sentences should be framed in such a way that they convey only one meaning and not several.

Scenario 2 : He ate the biscuits on the bed.
This sentence can mean –
1) the biscuits which were on the bed were eaten by him.
2) he ate the biscuits sitting on the bed.

Thus, sentences should be framed in such a way that they convey only one meaning and not several.

Scenario 3 : He gave her cat food.
This sentence can mean –
1) he gave food to her cat.
2) cat food was given to her.

Thus, sentences should be framed in such a way that they convey only one meaning and not several.
